Implementations of conditions for filtering the frequency histogram

Structs

  • LastNItems
    A general condition for checking the previous N items. Can take any vector of items and filters for accesses in the trace where the last N items were the exact contents of the vector.
  • LastNStrides
    A general condition for checking the previous N strides. Can take any vector of strides (i64) and filters for accesses in the trace where the last N strides were the exact contents of the vector.
  • NoCondition
    No condition on the trace, includes all elements for a full frequency histogram
